Who are we?

We are group of sport enthusiasts that come together on a regular basis to play Floorball. The game allows a fun and exciting way to exercise and promote better health.

Since we started our team we've enjoyed all the benefits of this great sport and continue to encourage others to join us as well.

 

 

What is Floorball?

Fast paced, safe and low-cost

Like indoor hockey but played with light-weight fiberglass or carbon fibre sticks

Played in many countries around the world

Rapidly growing and focus is on speed and skill

Contender as an Olympic sport

 

Benefits of Floorball

Exciting sport for all age groups

Physically builds endurance, strength and agility

Low cost and easy to learn

Promotes great social camaraderie
